Connecting to Peers

by BlakeKeiller, Wednesday, June 10, 2020, 07:56 (543 days ago)

A small list of nodes to connect to are here:

https://proofgold.org/peers.txt

If your Proofgold node cannot find a peer, it will fallback on downloading the list at this url and try these.

You can also explicitly add connections with the "addnode" command. For example, you could do this:

addnode 139.59.21.170:21805

The command "getpeerinfo" will show the current list of connections and the last time each connection passed a message.
