Proofgold 0.1.8 (Emergency Hard Fork)

by BlakeKeiller, Sunday, August 30, 2020, 15:14 (462 days ago)

Proofgold 0.1.8 is available:

https://proofgold.org/proofgold-0.1.8.tgz

Everyone must update since the consensus rules have changed. I have created one new block under the new rules and will stake slowly for the next 24 hours while others update and restart their nodes.

The primary change is fixing the bug in the calculation of Diophantine problems. I also expanded the number of assets allowed at an address from 32 to 34. (Otherwise the bounties at the two address with 32 address could technically never be claimed.) The number of bounties at an address is still limited to 32.

As this was an emergency, the code has not undergone much testing. Please post something or send me a pm if you notice something is wrong.
