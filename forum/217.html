Blockchain alternative to the forum

by BlakeKeiller, Friday, November 26, 2021, 13:15 (9 days ago) @ Brown

Block chain based forums are not a new idea. There have been plenty of them. One bitcoin cash based one is memo.cash and on the front page of proofgold.org I link to a "proofgold" account on memo.cash I control:

https://memo.cash/profile/1NzEUQWpb5Mze9REfkVAZ8wDcxzqpZFNJ8

There's no activity there and my last post was almost a year ago. Still, if I ever should need to make a post that is "provably me" in the future, I can make the post with that memo.cash account.

Another example is https://member.cash/ which I think is just a different interface to the same bitcoin cash based messaging as memo.cash. Here's the same account via member.cash:

https://member.cash/#member?qaddress=8155342699537182388

Generally I would strongly encourage people to adopt a system like these. That said, I hardly use them myself, as everyone can see.
