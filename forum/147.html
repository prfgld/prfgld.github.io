New Bounties

by BlakeKeiller, Sunday, January 24, 2021, 20:54 (315 days ago)

20 new bounties from the bounty reward fund have been placed. All the propositions are again Ramsey problems, specifically dealing with the values of R(3,6), R(4,5), R(3,7), R(4,6) and R(5,5).
